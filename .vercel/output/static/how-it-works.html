Purpose of MindMash

MindMash is an AI-driven knowledge management tool designed to help you organize information and connect ideas efficiently.

  • •
    Organize ideas, tasks, and notes as visual knowledge graphs
  • •
    AI assistance for automatic tagging, suggestions, and connections
  • •
    Capture information via text, voice, or document imports
  • •
    Structure information with supertags like #task or #project
  • •
    Share and collaborate on knowledge graphs with your team

Four Simple Steps to Get Started

1

Capture

Type notes, record voice memos, or import Zoom conversations. Our AI automatically transcribes and organizes everything.

  • •Type text directly into the app
  • •Record voice memos for automatic transcription
  • •Import documents, images, and files
2

Tag

Use #task, #project, or create custom tags to instantly structure your information and trigger AI processing.

  • •Use hashtags like #task, #project, #idea
  • •Create custom tags for your specific needs
  • •AI automatically suggests relevant tags
3

Connect

The knowledge graph automatically links related information, helping you discover connections you might have missed.

  • •Drag and connect related information
  • •Get AI suggestions for potential connections
  • •Automatic linking of related content
4

Collaborate

Share nodes or entire graphs with your team, enabling seamless collaboration and knowledge sharing.

  • •Share specific nodes with team members
  • •Invite team members to collaborate
  • •Real-time editing and commenting

Quick Start Tips

  1. createAccount - Sign up for free and set up your profile.
  2. Create your first node - Type a note, import a document, or record your voice.
  3. Add tags - Use #project or #task to automatically categorize your content.
  4. Connect nodes - Drag and drop to connect related information in your graph.
  5. Explore AI features - Use AI to generate ideas, summarize content, and find connections.

Pro Tip:

Use keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Space (or Cmd+Space on Mac) to quickly create a new node anywhere in your graph.
